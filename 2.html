Download and Install WinRAR

Follow these steps to download and install WinRAR on your computer:

Step 1: Download WinRAR

1. Visit the official WinRAR website at https://www.win-rar.com/.

2. Navigate to the "Downloads" section and choose the appropriate version for your operating system (32-bit or 64-bit).

3. Click on the download link to start the download process.

Step 2: Install WinRAR

1. Locate the downloaded installer file and double-click to run it.

2. Follow the on-screen instructions to install WinRAR on your computer.

3. After installation, you can use WinRAR to create and extract compressed archives.
